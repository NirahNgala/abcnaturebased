Our Services

Why Projects Struggle in Execution

Poor Seedling Quality

Low-quality seedlings reduce survival rates and lead to repeated replanting.

Fragmented Contracting

Multiple contractors without central oversight cause inconsistent results.

Insufficient Maintenance

Inadequate care and follow-up affect long-term project success.

Lack of Supervision

Low monitoring leads to unpredictable outcomes, especially in drylands.

Our Solution: ABC removes execution risk by taking full operational responsibility for your projects.

What We Deliver

Operational delivery you can outsource

ABC enables clients to outsource major components of project execution, allowing them to focus on project development, finance, certification and stakeholder engagement.

Seedling Supply and Nursery Operations

ABC produces and supplies high-quality, dryland-adapted seedlings through its own nurseries. Clients do not need to design, finance or manage nurseries themselves. We deliver planting material that meets project specifications, volumes and timelines.

Planting, Maintenance and Field Management

ABC manages planting campaigns, early establishment and long-term maintenance using trained field teams, appropriate mechanisation, and site-specific water and protection systems. We take responsibility for survival optimisation across planting seasons and years.

Operational Monitoring and Supervision

Field supervision and operational data collection are integrated into daily operations. ABC manages on-the-ground monitoring to support project management and client reporting requirements.
